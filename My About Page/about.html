My Life as a Football Player

One of the most significant activities in my life would have to be football ever since I got a hold of the ball in 5th grade, it sparked my interest and a fire was ignited.

Ever since I was a child, I've been captivated by the world of football. The thrill of the game, the roar of the crowd, and the sheer beauty of a perfectly executed play have always stirred something deep within me. Football is more than just a game to me; it's a passion, a dream, and a path I long to follow.

Why I Choose Football?

Soccer has kept me off the streets, it keeps me focused, maintaining agility, stamina and a side of aggression. The feeling I get when playing soccer is indescribable, the wind in my face, the crowd cheering me on..most importantly the goals.

soccer relates so much to my everyday life, it taught me teamwork and keeping myself healthy and sharp. The one person that i look up to is Mesut Ozil. The way he plays the ball...He make it so simple and exquisite of which his enthusiasm really drove me to play a beautiful game like him.
